A repeatable in process load test against a stubbed upstream (no network, no spend). Throughput is reported as goodput, the rate of successful responses, kept separate from offered load and from shed requests. Client side numbers reconcile against the proxy's own metrics.

Goodput vs offered

Useful work, not raw acceptance

Goodput is successful responses per second. Where it sits below offered load, the gap is either shed (429) or queued. A healthy overloaded system holds goodput near capacity instead of letting everything degrade.
